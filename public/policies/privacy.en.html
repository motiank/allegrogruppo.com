1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy and Terms of Use ("Policy") apply to the online ordering website operated by EATALIA ("the restaurant", "we", "us"). By using our website and placing an order, you agree to this Policy, including the collection, processing, and use of personal data as described here.

This document is written in masculine grammatical form only for convenience and applies equally to all users.

2. Types of Information We Collect

2.1 Information Provided by the User

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Delivery address (if delivery is requested)
  • Order details: selected dishes, preferences, customization requests
  • Any additional notes provided voluntarily, including dietary preferences and sensitivities

2.2 Payment Information

Payments on this website are processed by a certified external payment provider. EATALIA does not store or have access to your credit card details.

2.3 Automatically Collected Technical Information

  • IP address
  • Device type and browser
  • Operating system
  • Pages viewed and session duration
  • Error and performance logs (for security and maintenance)

2.4 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies (including Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, and Meta/Facebook Pixel) to enable website functionality, save the shopping cart, analyze usage, and improve the service. Cookies may be blocked or deleted via browser settings; however, blocking cookies may limit some site features.

Local Storage: The website uses browser local storage to store an anonymous user identifier for the purpose of analyzing user behavior and improving our service. This identifier does not contain personally identifiable information and is randomly generated when you first load the page. The identifier is stored in your browser and remains persistent across different visits to the site, allowing us to track user behavior over time and improve the user experience. You can delete information stored in local storage through your browser settings.

3. Purpose of Data Use

  1. Processing orders and transferring them to the restaurant
  2. Contacting the customer when needed (order confirmation, updates)
  3. Improving and personalizing user experience
  4. Statistical analysis and performance reporting
  5. Fraud prevention, security monitoring, and system protection
  6. Compliance with legal and regulatory requirements

No use of personal data will be made beyond these purposes unless we receive explicit consent or are required by law.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

EATALIA does not sell personal information to third parties.

Information may be shared with:

  • Payment processing providers
  • Technology vendors (hosting, analytics, security)
  • Restaurant management systems to fulfill the order
  • Government authorities only when required by law

5. Data Retention & Security

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary for providing services, complying with legal obligations, and fulfilling our business needs. Security measures are implemented to protect data from unauthorized access; however, no online platform can guarantee absolute security.

No payment data is stored on our systems.

6. User Rights

Under applicable law, you may request access, correction, or deletion of personal information. Identity verification may be required when processing such requests.

Requests may be sent to: SUPPORT@ALLEGROGRUPPO.COM

7. Marketing & Communications

Service messages (such as order confirmations) may be sent automatically. Promotional messages will only be sent with your explicit consent and may be opted out of at any time.

8. Minors

This website is intended for users aged 18 and above. If personal data is discovered to have been submitted by a minor, it will be removed to the extent possible after notification.

9. Terms of Use

  • The website is intended for personal use and non-transferable.
  • Prices may change without prior notice; product images (if displayed) are for illustration only.
  • An order is considered accepted only once final confirmation is provided.
  • EATALIA may refuse or cancel an order due to errors, stock availability, or other circumstances.

10. Changes to Policy

EATALIA reserves the right to update this Policy at any time. The latest update date appears at the top of the document. Continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
